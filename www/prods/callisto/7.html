6.2.1
Early post-emergence application of CALLISTO plus
FALCON GOLD plus DIURON 800 SC plus a recom-
mended adjuvant on sugarcane.
1)

CALLISTO
is not affected by the clay
content of the soil.
2)
The application of
CALLISTO
plus FALCON GOLD plus
DIURON 800 SC plus Complement Super must be applied
early post-emergence of the weeds.
3)
At application, the growth stage of the
• grasses should be between the 2 and 3 leaves without any
tillers.
• broadleaf weeds should be between 4 and 6 leaf stage.
4)
Recommended adjuvants:
In all post emergence applica-
tions of
CALLISTO
an adjuvant must be used. The following
adjuvants are recommended :
• Complement Super teen 100 m
l
/ha
• Tronic at 500 m
l
/ha
5)
Applications should be made during the late spring and sum-
mer season when the chance of rain is good.
6)
CALLISTO

or little rain is expected.
7)
Drip irrigation is not sufficient to activate
CALLISTO
in the
absence of rainfall.

CALLISTO

9)
