to three applications

needed.

Stem treatment:
Start to monitor for weevils
from
beginning
October
and
continue
monitoring
throughout
the
growing
season.
Apply
as
a
preventive
stem
treatment
as soon as weevil activity is
noticed on the stem. Apply in
1 000
l
spray mixture/ha (at
least 0,5
l
/plant) up to a
height of 1 m. In the case
of low growing vines (trees),
spray up to the first lateral
branches.
Wet the stem thoroughly and
allow excess spray mixture to
run onto the soil surrounding
the stem. Repeat application
3 4 weeks later if necessary.
If a dense canopy has already
formed which can serve as
a hiding place for weevils
which will prevent them from
migrating back to the stem
during the day, follow the
foliar application below but
first ensure that an infestation
is present in the foliage prior
to application.
